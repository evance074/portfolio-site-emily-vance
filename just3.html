Just3

Track your progress on the 3 factors most important to YOU!

Just3 offers an easy-to-understand, personalized quiz that evaluates a user's current health and wellness status and offers tailored insights into where the user should focus.

THE CHALLENGE

People struggle to stay motivated and track long-term health progress.

THE OPPORTUNITY

Our health conscious user needs a way to identify and track long-term health goals!

THE SOLUTION

Just3 simplifies this journey by providing a personalized quiz, personalized insights and easy goal tracking.

THE DESIGN PROCESS

Double Diamond Design

I used the Double Diamond approach in my UX case study because it offers a structured way to address complex problems. By researching the problem space, I gained insights into user needs before defining the core challenge. In the solution phase, I iterated and tested designs to create a user-centered outcome, balancing creativity with strategy.

DISCOVER

User Research

Exploratory user interviews uncovered valuable insights

  • Users are thinking about the bigger picture and long-term health
  • Users find it challenging to stay motivated
  • Users are overwhelmed and anxious about how to stay healthy
  • Users are pro-technology

Data and Analysis

Prototype Iterations

Moderated usability tests revealed key insights into usability, design, and overall experience. Feedback helped identify pain points and guide improvements, making the app more intuitive and engaging.

Here's how user input shaped each iteration!

Users were unsure about their goals and were confused by the calendar function (high priority)

  • Moved from a calendar home page to a “recent history” model.
  • Created a “goal card” at the top to display a user's status.
  • Highlighted the streak functionality.
  • Included a new additional call to action button - “New Entry”

Users expected the notebook logo to be something else (high priority)

  • Version 1: Directed the notebook logo to the log history instead of quiz results and relocate the quiz to the account settings.
  • Version 2: For additional clarity, version 2 replaced the current data logo with a line graph logo.
  • Version 3: Adjusted navigation bar to align with Material Design 3 best practices, maintaining the icon design.
  • Version 4: Relocated the “New Entry” button to position 2 due to its importance.

Final Designs

Just3 has evolved significantly through continuous iteration and user feedback.

We began by identifying a business need, then conducted competitive analysis to spark ideas. Through in-depth user research, we uncovered key challenges—like feeling overwhelmed and struggling with motivation—helping us validate our approach and refine the design.

Guided by user insights, we built mental models, mapped tasks, and developed wireframes at various fidelities, exploring multiple design options.

Usability and preference tests highlighted key improvements, leading to major refinements—especially in onboarding and the homepage. Continuous testing and iteration helped shape a more user-friendly, accessible, and engaging app.
